## Measuring chlorophyll from symbionts

### Materials needed 

- 100% acetone
- Centrifuge
- P1000 + tips
- Automatic pipette + tips 
- Microplate reader 
- 96 well plate (plastic)

### Protocol 

- Remove 15mL tubes with the symbiont pellets from the -80C freezer. Thaw the pellets on the benchtop. 
- Once pellets are thawed, add 9mL of 100% acetone to the pellets and vortex until the pellet is broken up. 
- Store samples upright in the dark at -20C for 48 hrs. 
	- Wrap samples in foil if needed.
- After 48 hrs, centrifuge samples at max speed for 3 mins at 4C.
- Pipette triplicate 300 uL of supernatent per sample into a 96 well plate. 
	- As you go, cover the filled wells with parafilm to reduce evaporation.
	- The blank will be also run in triplicate and will be 100% acetone. 
- Bring plate to Polschoder lab and read plate at absorbances 630, 663, and 750nm. 
- Let plate evaporate in fume hood before cleaning it with DI water. 
- Pour acetone samples into labeled hazardous waste container.
